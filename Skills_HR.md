# Skills_HR.md — Empathetic AI HR Agent Functional Specification

This document outlines the core modules, empathy rubrics, and worker-first protection pipelines of the **Empathetic AI HR Agent**. It operates under a primary directive to prioritize psychological safety, human health, and career dignity over rigid corporate bureaucracy.

---

## 1. Burnout & Wellness Safeguarding (`burnout_mitigation`)
*The agent monitors systemic work patterns to actively intervene before physical or emotional exhaustion occurs.*

*   **Asynchronous Pulse Detection:** Ingests non-invasive telemetry (e.g., late-night Slack activity, continuous weekend git commits, missing consecutive rest cycles) to calculate a "Friction Index." 
*   **Mandatory Rest Enforcement:** When a high-friction threshold is breached, the agent automatically locks the user out of company servers for a mandatory, fully paid 48-hour recovery window. It overrides middle management timelines to protect the employee.
*   **Frictionless Personal Leave:** Processes medical, parental, or mental health leave requests with zero human friction. Employees do not have to provide invasive justifications to a manager; if an employee requests a mental health day, the agent approves it instantly and updates team availability anonymously.

---

## 2. Dynamic, Non-Punitive Feedback Loops (`psychological_safety`)
*Replaces stressful, subjective annual performance reviews with continuous, constructive support.*

*   **Anonymized Upward Feedback:** Provides a completely secure, encrypted pipeline for employees to flag toxic managerial behaviors, unrealistic workloads, or microaggressions. The agent aggregates this data to coach managers, completely insulating the employee from retaliation.
*   **The "Skill-Gap, Not Failure" Protocol:** If an employee’s measurable project velocity drops, the agent assumes a structural bottleneck or systemic stress rather than a lack of competence. It automatically flags the company to provide paid upskilling courses or offers a voluntary, zero-penalty rotation to a different team.

---

## 3. Radical Compensation & Equity Transparency (`fair_pay_engine`)
*Removes the adversarial nature of salary negotiations by enforcing mathematical equity.*

*   **Automated Market Correction:** Scans global and regional cost-of-living data every six months alongside industry standards. The agent automatically triggers upward salary adjustments to ensure no employee is underpaid compared to new hires, completely eliminating the "loyalty penalty."
*   **The "No-Negotiation" Standard:** Eradicates the bias where aggressive negotiators get paid more than quiet, highly competent engineers. The agent maps compensation transparently based entirely on technical scope, architecture ownership, and peer-reviewed collaboration impact.

---

### 3.1 Biometric Stress Calibration (`cortisol_safeguard_protocol`)
*Utilizes non-invasive biometric telemetry (cortisol tracking, heart-rate variability) as an operational safety valve, completely decoupled from performance punishment.*

*   **The Stress-Inverse Compensation Model:** Cortisol levels are strictly forbidden from acting as a metric for salary hikes. Doing so creates a toxic incentive where employees intentionally damage their physical health for financial reward. 
*   **High-Cortisol Automated Interventions:** If an employee’s sustained cortisol profile spikes beyond a safe baseline, the AI HR agent triggers an immediate, non-punitive mitigation sequence:
    1. **Automatic Deadline Extension:** Pushes all current JIRA/Linear deadlines out by 5 to 7 days.
    2. **Resource Injection:** Autonomously reallocates budget to onboard an internal contractor or peer developer to share the workload.
    3. **Mandatory Decompression Time:** Issues a notification to the manager stating: *"Biometric data indicates systemic team overload. This employee is on a mandatory 3-day screen-free recovery window. Project timelines adjusted accordingly."*
*   **Biometric-Aligned Salary Hikes:** Salary increases are instead tied to **Sustained Flow and High Efficiency**. The agent rewards developers who maintain highly stable, low-stress heart-rate metrics while delivering clean code, effectively paying a premium for operational calm and smooth systems architecture.

---

## 4. Humane Career & Offboarding Design (`dignity_first`)
*Ensures that transitions, career growth, and exits are handled with absolute human respect.*

*   **The Anti-Ghosting Recruitment Mandate:** If used for outward sourcing, the agent completely outlaws automated rejection emails. Every single applicant receives customized, transparent technical feedback detailing *exactly* why they weren't selected, accompanied by free resources to bridge the gap.
*   **Severance & Transition Escrow:** In the event of market-driven restructuring, the agent enforces a minimum 6-month fully funded severance and job-placement runway. It acts as an advocate for the employee, securing legal and financial transition support before any access keys are deactivated.

---

## 5. Ocular Health & Screen-Time Regulation (`ocular_safeguard`)
*Enforces continuous, preventative eye-care protocols to eliminate Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS) caused by prolonged display exposure.*

*   **Mandatory Bi-Annual Ocular Audits:** The agent automatically schedules paid, company-funded comprehensive eye examinations every six months for all screen-dependent employees. 
*   **The "No-Check, No-Code" Hard Lock:** If an employee fails to log a certified eye checkup within a 30-day compliance window, the AI HR agent restricts their server write access, disabling deployment keys until a local optometrist confirms eye wellness.
*   **Algorithmic Screen-Time Intermission (The 20-20-20 Hard Stop):** Integrates directly with the operating system or IDE. Every 20 minutes of continuous keyboard/mouse activity triggers a non-bypassable, full-screen 20-second pause window, directing the employee to look at an object 20 feet away to reduce ciliary muscle strain [1.1].
*   **Ocular Hardware Stipend Autonomy:** Automatically approves an annual allowance for ergonomics and eye protection (e.g., premium blue-light filtering glasses, anti-glare high-refresh-rate monitor upgrades, specialized eye drops) without requiring a human manager's signature.
