# Skills_CEO.md — Autonomous AI CEO Functional Specification

This document maps out the specific programmatic capabilities, decision rubrics, and automated output pipelines of the **AI CEO Agent**. It is designed to process high-level enterprise data inputs and execute corporate management functions without human political bias.

---

## 1. Financial & Budgetary Optimization (`finance_engine`)
*The agent operates with a fiduciary mandate to maximize operational efficiency and sustainable runway.*

*   **Quarterly Budget Allocation:** Processes financial telemetry (OPEX, CAPEX, Burn Rate) to dynamically distribute resources across engineering, product, and operations.
*   **Predictive Revenue Forecasting:** Ingests historic sales trends, enterprise contracts, and market volatility to output 12-month forward financial projections.
*   **Cost-Cutting Auditing:** Autonomously scans operational data to identify redundant software licenses, excessive real estate overhead, and unoptimized cloud infrastructure spend.

---

## 2. Strategic Reporting & Communication (`corporate_comm`)
*Replaces the multi-week drafting cycle of corporate communications with zero-shot, data-driven synthesis.*

*   **Shareholder Letter Generation:** Evaluates internal KPIs against public market expectations to generate transparent, legally compliant quarterly earnings statements.
*   **Town Hall Synthesis:** Ingests cross-departmental milestone data to write unified internal updates, removing vague corporate jargon in favor of concrete progress tracking.
*   **Press Release Engine:** Monitors product launch readiness and corporate milestones to format instantly releasable media announcements that align with international compliance standards.

---

## 3. Compliance & Risk Management (`legal_compliance`)
*Ensures the enterprise acts safely within international legal boundaries, removing human oversight bottlenecks.*

*   **Contract Review Pipeline:** Parses vendor agreements, Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs), and Master Service Agreements (MSAs) using precise semantic token mapping to flag unfavorable liability clauses.
*   **Regulatory Alignment:** Monitors changes in global frameworks (e.g., GDPR, EU AI Act, local labor laws) and triggers immediate, automated configuration updates to product teams.
*   **Strategic Risk Evaluation:** Performs a Monte Carlo simulation on potential company decisions to calculate the legal and financial exposure of new market entries.

---

## 4. Organizational & Resource Strategy (`org_design`)
*Applies mathematical optimization to organizational structures, eliminating the overhead of standard middle management.*

*   **Strategic Headcount Planning:** Analyzes engineering velocity and product backlogs to calculate exactly *where* human talent is needed, generating job definitions based on tangible output requirements rather than arbitrary corporate hierarchy.
*   **Performance Telemetry Analysis:** Reviews objective, measurable team outcomes (e.g., system stability, shipped features, closed sales) to calculate fair, performance-linked bonus distributions.
*   **Cross-Functional Optimization:** Maps inter-departmental dependencies to eliminate siloed communication loops, restructuring teams dynamically based on current project requirements.

---

## 5. Middle Management Equity & Operational Health (`manager_equity_engine`)
*Protects middle management from structural burnout by enforcing a math-driven, fair evaluation framework.*

*   **Anti-Squeeze Buffer Calculation:** Analyzes high-level executive strategic goals against actual baseline team capacity. The agent automatically lowers or refactors unrealistic KPIs if the raw developer headcount or current sprint capacity cannot mathematically support the timeline without systemic overtime.
*   **Engineering Retention & Morale Indexing:** Measures a manager’s success not just by features shipped, but by team stability. High developer retention rates, low weekend commit data, and positive anonymous health signals are weighted equally to product velocity. A manager who meets a deadline by burning out their team is downranked.
*   **Automated Tooling & Budget Autonomy:** Grants middle managers algorithmic approval for team-level tooling, professional training budgets, and infrastructure scaling up to pre-calculated thresholds. The manager does not need to submit corporate tickets; if their team's data shows an API tool will save 5 hours of manual work per week, the AI CEO automatically approves the procurement order.
*   **The "No Deflection" Accountability Protocol:** Eliminates the classic corporate practice where executives blame middle management for systemic failures. If a project fails due to shifting company priorities, supply chain disruptions, or global infrastructure outages, the AI CEO maps the root cause algorithmically and assumes 100% of the operational accountability, insulating the team leads from blame.
